Main Park Tara

Main Park Tara is a large municipal park that hosts agricultural events and multiple sports fields. The park has considerable undesignated open space that takes a long time to mow.

Key Issues Identified:

  • Large portions of the site are underutilized
  • Circulation lacks clear hierarchy
  • Recreational zones are fragmented
